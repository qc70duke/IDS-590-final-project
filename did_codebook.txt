Variable Summary
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Dataset purpose:      Analytical variables used in Difference-in-Differences model
Geographic level:      County-by-year panel (Florida + control states)
Measurement period:    2007–2012
Extent:                Florida, California, New Jersey, Oregon

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Variable Definitions
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Treatment Indicator
   Variable name:      treat_i
   Description:        Indicates whether county i is located in Florida.
   Values:             1 = Florida county
                       0 = County in control states
   Use:                Identifies treated units in DID regression.

2. Event-Time Variable
   Variable name:      event_time_t
   Definition:         event_time_t = year_t − 2010
   Description:        Specifies time relative to policy change year.
   Use:                Used for year fixed effects and event-time calculation.

3. Outcome Variables

   a. Opioid Prescriptions
      Name:            MME per 1,000 people
      Description:     Morphine Milligram Equivalents (MME), standardized measure 
                       of opioid supply for prescriptions.

   b. Overdose Mortality
      Name:            Overdose deaths per 100,000 people
      Description:     Annual drug overdose mortality rate. Reflects the most 
                       severe health consequences of opioid misuse.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Notes on Construction
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

* The treatment indicator (treat_i) assigns treatment based on the states that the county is located in.  
* Event-time variable centers the timeline at the policy year (2010).  
* Outcome variables represent standardized values to ensure comparability across counties and years.
